Game of Thrones has intrigue.

They say the pen is mightier than the sword, but can they back it up? Watch as characters with little to no power use their wits to topple larger than life foes with nothing but the words on their lips.

And it has action.

Sometimes negotians fall apart, or unpredictable circumstances upend otherwise solid plans. Sometimes passions run too high to control. Sometimes words just arent enough. In these situations action is the only option and you'll find loads of action in Game of Thrones.

And most importantly, it has Dragons.

The "children" of Daenerys, dragons represent unimaginable power. thought to be extinct, Dragons dominate the armies of the Seven Kingdoms. Can any force ever hope to match the unfellable might of a dragon?
